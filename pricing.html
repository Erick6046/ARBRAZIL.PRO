Frequently Asked Questions

What are surebets and how do they guarantee profit?

Surebets occur when the combined odds from different bookmakers create a situation where you can place bets on all possible outcomes and guarantee a profit regardless of the result. Our system identifies these mathematical opportunities in real-time.

How often are new surebets discovered?

Our system discovers 200+ surebets daily during peak sports seasons, with opportunities available across all major sports and markets. The frequency varies by sport and season.

What profit margins can I expect?

Profit margins range from 5% to 12% per surebet, depending on the market conditions and bookmaker odds. Our Elite plan focuses on higher-margin opportunities (10%-12%).

Is surebetting legal?

Yes, surebetting is completely legal. You're simply taking advantage of odds differences between bookmakers. However, bookmakers may limit accounts that consistently win, so proper stake management is important.
